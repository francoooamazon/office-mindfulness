Mindful Moment · 03

Body Scan

Spend 3–5 minutes moving your attention gently through your body. Notice without judgment.

Head & Face
Are your jaw and forehead relaxed? Soften any tension you find.
Neck & Shoulders
Let your shoulders drop. Release any tightness in your neck.
Chest & Breath
Notice your chest rising and falling. Take one slow, deep breath.
Arms & Hands
Unclench your hands. Let your arms feel heavy and at ease.
Hips & Lower Back
Notice where you're sitting. Is there tension? Adjust if needed.
Legs & Feet
Feel your feet on the floor. Ground yourself in this moment.
