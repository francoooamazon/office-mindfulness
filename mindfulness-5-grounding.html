Grounding Technique

Feeling anxious or overwhelmed? Use your senses to anchor yourself to the present moment.

5
👁 See
Name 5 things you can see right now. Look around slowly and really notice each one.
4
✋ Touch
Find 4 things you can physically feel — the chair beneath you, your feet on the floor, fabric, temperature.
3
👂 Hear
Listen for 3 distinct sounds. Near or far. Notice sounds you usually tune out.
2
👃 Smell
Identify 2 things you can smell. Take a slow breath through your nose and notice what's there.
1
👅 Taste
Notice 1 thing you can taste, or take a sip of water and focus entirely on that sensation.
